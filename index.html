About Us

The way we live, the things we consume, the means we use to get around all of these things we do in our daily lives have a huge impact on our planet. According to WHO Carbon footprint is the amount of gases that are generated by our actions .The united States holds the number one spot for highest carbon footprint generated per capita in the world.

Our goal is to inform you about what carbon footprint is and help you determine what is the amount of carbon footprint generated by your daily activities , we will help you create a benchmark and keep a record of your footprint as well as help you understand your emissions.
